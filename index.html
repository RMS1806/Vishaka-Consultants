Welcome to VISHAKA CONSULTANTS

Creating a pathway towards diversity, equanimity, and harmony at the workplace!

“Embracing Unity: Empowering a world of differences”!

VISHAKA CONSULTANTS is a social enterprise that aims to create a pathway that leads to diversity, equanimity, and harmony in the workplace. It embraces unity and empowers a world of differences!

Cultivating Workplace Well-being is Important!

In the hustle and bustle of the workweek, it is crucial to prioritize the well-being of every employee. A positive culture can make work a pleasure, achievable through a tranquil work environment, diversity, and equanimity.

A workplace that prioritizes these values can benefit all employees and build a strong organizational structure in the long run.

Our Core Values

How diversity, equanimity, and harmony are beneficial at the workplace.

Diversity

Brings a wealth of perspectives, ideas, and experiences that drive innovation and creativity, enabling companies to better understand and serve their diverse customer base.

Equanimity

Ensures all employees are treated with fairness and respect, regardless of their background or position. This balance is crucial for preventing discrimination and promoting belonging.

Harmony

Enhances collaboration and reduces conflict, leading to a more cohesive and supportive work culture. Valued employees work together effectively towards the organization’s goals.

POSH: Ensuring a Safe Workplace

To safeguard and retain women at the workplace, the POSH Act (Prevention of Sexual Harassment) plays a significantly supportive role. When women feel safe, supported, and validated, they are more likely to pursue their dreams and contribute their unique talents.

We, at VISHAKA, strongly follow and recommend the implementation of the POSH Act!

About VISHAKA CONSULTANTS

Creating a pathway towards a diversified, equanimous and harmonious workplace for goodwill and increased business returns!

Building the Stairway!

Founded in 2018 by social entrepreneur Rachna Saksena, Vishaka is a hybrid (non-profit and profit) organisation striving to create equanimity and a favourably harmonious, diversified environment at the workplace.

By collaborating with government organizations and providing dedicated guidance and capacity building, Vishaka aims to create awareness and sustainable solutions that lead to a level playing field.

Bridging the Gap!

Vishaka is committed to building the stairway by bridging the gap at policy, organizational, and individual levels through the following steps:

ADVOCACY!

  • Promoting gender-positive policies.
  • Opinion building through dialogue & research.
  • Gender budgeting in public policy.

SYSTEMIC INTERVENTIONS!

  • Workshops on gender sensitization.
  • Audit and assessment of affirmative action.
  • External member on IC committees (POSH).

DIVERSITY TALENT ENGAGEMENT!

  • Capacity building for the changing workplace.
  • Coaching and counselling for performance.
  • Developing women leaders.

Our Vision!

With a vision of a diverse, harmonious, and inclusive ecosystem at the workplace, Vishaka aims to inspire and bring about positive change in the lives of countless individuals, leaving an indelible impact on the communities it serves!
